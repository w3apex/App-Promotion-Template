What do I need to start

Registration Requirements

Accept Vehicles

We only accept motorcycles with engine capacity of 125cc and above in good working and physical condition

Inspection

Before motorcycle is accepted into the Okadagh network, we carry out physical inspection to ensure it meets our operating standards. Safety and digital literacy - ability to use and understands the app.

Documentation

The following documentation is required for every motorcycle in our network;

  • Proof of ownership or consent of owner
  • Valid DVLA Registration - Roadworthy Certificate
  • Valid Insurance Cover

Safety & Digital Literacy

Our Deliverymen must meet the following requirements too

  • Safe Helmet & boots
  • Ability to use and understans the app
