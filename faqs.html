FAQ's

Help & support

Who are the Okada?

Okada is an on-demand application that allow customers to order from their favourite vendor and have it delivered seamlessly at their preferred location. It integrates e-commerce and delivery service in a special way to offer convenience to the customer. The service features 5 key categories – Food, Delivery, Shopping, Pharmacy, Gifts, and Drinks. Additional categories will be added in due course

The service is currently available in Accra and Tema. We shall be launching soon in other cities. Watch out!

Note: The app is multi lingual – supports English, French and Arabic. You simply switch to your preferred language from your settings

How much does the service cost?

We charge a small delivery fee for the orders based on distance and on the agreement with every store. You can see this in the display of each order you make.

Is Okada available in my neighborhood?

The service is currently available in Accra and Tema. We shall be launching soon in other cities. Watch out!

What are the opening hours?

Okada is open all day! Open stores are available for you to shop from and closed stores have “closed” on them until they are opening times.

Can I schedule an order?

Of course! You just have to choose when you want it delivered before confirming your order, and you will receive it when it is best for you!

What can I ask for?

Use the app to buy any product or make your deliveries and express messages in the city. Do you want us to go to the pharmacy for you? Do we buy a pair of shoes? Would you like something to eat?

Did you forget your keys at home? Anything you want. Delivered in minutes.

How I can find what I want?

At the top of the screen, there is a search engine that lets you find any product or establishment of the app in your city.

Methods of payments and incidents

When will my order arrive?

As long as you have an assigned Okada you can follow it in real time and communicate with your deliveryman for any questions. If you do not have an okada deliveryman assigned, your order remains in the system pending allocation.

There are missing or wrong products in the order

If you have received an incomplete or wrong order, contact support@okadagh.com and we will get back to you promptly with a solution.

The order has not arrived in good condition

If the order has not arrived in good condition contact us at support@okadagh and we will answer you right away with a solution.

I want to cancel my order

Do you need to cancel it? You can cancel anytime you wish, but if you already have your food or product prepared you cannot cancel.

I didn’t order this. What do I do?

What happened? Does it correspond to the description of the product made? We wish we convey it to avoid situations like this in the future. In the specific case of orders placed on the Platform through the drop down of any store products, the customer should contact Okada directly.

What type of bank cards do you accept?

We accept Cash, Mobile money, Visa and MasterCard.

How do I make the payment?

The payment takes place through the application and only once the service is performed.

Is it safe to add my credit card?

Our system of payment through the app is completely safe. In order to ensure your security, we avoid storing any banking data.
